Welcome to Your Remote Wellness Hub

Working from home is great, but it comes with unique challenges like screen strain, isolation, and blurred boundaries. This site offers simple tips, exercises, and resources to help you stay healthy and productive.

Featured Tip: Quick Desk Stretch

Stand up every hour, reach for the sky, and hold for 10 seconds. Repeat 3 times to ease back tension.
